About BlackTieCars

Learn about our company, our mission, and the team that makes it all happen.

Our Story

BlackTieCars was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide the most reliable, luxurious, and professional transportation service in College Station and beyond. What started as a small operation with just two vehicles has grown into a premier transportation company with a diverse fleet of luxury vehicles.

Our founder, James Wilson, recognized the need for a transportation service that combined luxury, reliability, and exceptional customer service. Having worked in the hospitality industry for over a decade, James understood the importance of creating memorable experiences for clients.

Safety

What Sets Us Apart

At BlackTieCars, we believe that transportation should be more than just getting from point A to point B. It should be an experience that enhances your journey, whether you're heading to an important business meeting, a special event, or the airport.

Our attention to detail sets us apart from other transportation services. From the immaculate condition of our vehicles to the professional appearance and conduct of our chauffeurs, every aspect of our service is designed to provide a premium experience.

We're available 24/7, understanding that transportation needs don't follow a 9-to-5 schedule. Our commitment to round-the-clock service ensures that whether you need an early morning airport pickup or late-night transportation after an event, BlackTieCars is ready to serve you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book transportation?

We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak times. For special events or group transportation, booking 1-2 weeks ahead is advisable.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled service receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee. Please contact us for specific details.

Do you provide transportation outside of College Station?

Yes, we provide transportation services throughout major Texas cities including Houston, Dallas, Austin, and San Antonio. Additional fees may apply for long-distance travel.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods. Corporate clients may also set up invoicing with approved credit.
